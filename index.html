About TechFest

TechFest 2026 is a two-day technical festival for college students to explore coding, robotics, and innovative projects. Participate in competitions, workshops, and hands-on sessions organized by our enthusiastic student and faculty team.

Event Tracks

Coding Challenge

Participate in problem-solving contests using Python, Java, or C++.

Robotics

Build and compete with simple autonomous robots.

Project Expo

Showcase innovative projects and prototypes developed by students.

Workshops

Hands-on workshops on web development, AI, and robotics.

Gaming

Friendly competitions for students in popular multiplayer games.

Quiz & Knowledge

Compete in fun technical and general knowledge quizzes.
